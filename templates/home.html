User Guide

This web app allows users to manage an inventory of products across multiple locations.

Users can add data in the form of products, locations, and stock.

Products have a brand, item name, and unit price.

Locations have a name and zip code.

Stock refers to the quantity of a given product at a location. Users can create stock which is assigned to a location.

Here's an example workflow to test. Image you are a skateboard retailer:

  1. Create a new location in your home town
  2. Create a new product the shop would carry
  3. Click 'add a new stock item' to declare that your shop is carrying some quantity of that product.

Users can view the inventory in a variety of ways:

  • View all products
  • View all locations
  • View all stock across locations
  • View all products at a given location
  • View all locations where a given product is in stock

Within each of these views, users have the ability to create, update, and delete stock.
